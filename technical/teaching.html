Teaching Experience

Graduate Teaching Assistant: Real Time Systems (CprE 558)

I had a great experience serving as a Teaching Assistant for Graduate level course Real Time Systems (CprE 558) in Fall 2020, Fall 2021 and Fall 20123 semesters for a class consisting of more than 50 students. My responsibilities were as follows:

1) One-to-one interaction with students for solving queries
2) Helping students in their final course project
3) Grading homeworks, exams, projects and providing appropriate feedback
4) Proctoring Midterm and Final exams

Graduate Teaching Assistant: Computer Engineering and Problem Solving (CprE 185) and Problem Solving in Software Engineering (SE 185)

I had an amazing experience serving as a Teaching Assistant for Undergraduate level course (CprE 185) and (SE 185) in Fall 2019 and Spring 2020 semesters for a class consisting of more than 200 students. My responsibilities were as follows:

1) Conducting labs every week to help students write code in C programming.
2) Helping students in their final course project
3) Holding office hours to facilitate one-to-one interaction for resolving queries
4) Grading homeworks, lab reports, exams and providing appropriate feedback
